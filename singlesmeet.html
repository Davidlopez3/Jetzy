About Event

Meet and hang out with the global group of jet setters. At Jetzy, we value creating connections and meeting new people as part of "Living The Jetzy Life" . This event provides an opportunity to connect with other singles in a time where travel and social activities are limited. Find someone you can connect with, find your next travel buddy and share your solo traveling stories. Make meaningful connections organically during the process that can lead to finding the love of your life!

You will fill out a short questionnaire that will be sent out closer to the event date and we will try to put you in groups with others based on your interests and compatibility. Must be over 18 to participate.

About Jetzy:

Jetzy is a global social network of explorers who have passion for travel and unique lifestyle experiences. Whether it's travel, food, entertainment or wellness, on our interest and location based Jetzy app you can contact like-minded people for authentic local recommendations.
